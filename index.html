Good for PoC

After thoughtful consideration, Good for PoC is transforming. We realized that all capitalist entities are inherently bad for people of color. Instead, we have decided to close the site and use the remaining funds to sponsor educational opportunities for people of color in design and technology. There's no catch – you just need to apply.
