Good for PoC

After thoughtful consideration, Good for PoC is transforming. We realized that all capitalist entities are inherently bad for people of color. Instead, we decided to close the site in September 2018 and use the remaining funds to sponsor educational opportunities for people of color in design and technology.

In total, we received 70 scholarship applicants. Their stories were varied and included freelancers struggling to find full-time work; event invitees in need of travel funding; and stay-at-home parents returning to work. A handful of recipients were selected in October 2018.
